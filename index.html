Our Cause

This fundraiser is giving money to Charity: Water. It is a non-profit organization that provides clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries

About Us

For years, small groups of children from all around the country have been organizing and performing in benefit concerts The proceeds from these concerts go to a charity chosen by the kids to benefit children in need. Due to COVID-19, we are doing things differently by creating a virtual benefit concert containing performances from all around the world. Even through our challenges, we are uniting to bring more good into the world. This is Children for Children.
